Why Join the Platform Team as a Graduate Engineer?

If you’re fresh out of university (or graduated in the last couple of years) and want to kick-start your career in tech, this is the place.

At LiveFlow, you won’t just write code - you’ll be part of a fast-paced, global team building real systems used by thousands of companies around the world.

You’ll learn by doing, working alongside experienced engineers while gaining exposure to modern infrastructure, internal tooling, and real-world production systems.

The Team You'll Work With

You’ll be based in our London office , working closely with our Head of Platform Engineering and two Platform Engineers already based there.

This means you’ll be surrounded by engineers who can support your learning and help you grow quickly as you start your career.

While our platform team has a strong presence in London, our wider engineering team is globally distributed across Europe, South America, and the United States. You’ll collaborate with teammates across time zones and gain exposure to how modern distributed engineering teams operate.

We work in a highly collaborative environment with regular pairing, code reviews, and knowledge sharing. As a graduate engineer, you’ll receive hands-on support while still having the opportunity to contribute to meaningful projects from day one.

What You’ll Work On

As a Graduate Platform Engineer, you’ll work on projects such as:

  • Building internal tools that improve productivity across engineering, product, and growth teams
  • Supporting the infrastructure behind our Growth Marketing Engine and Pulse platform
  • Contributing to data pipelines and integrations with external financial data providers
  • Developing APIs and backend services that support internal systems
  • Building internal AI tools that help teams automate workflows and move faster
  • Improving automation and developer tooling used across the engineering team
  • Helping ensure our systems remain secure, reliable, and scalable as we grow

You’ll work closely with experienced engineers and gain hands-on experience with the infrastructure and systems that power a fast-growing fintech.

Our Tech Stack

Backend: Elixir / Phoenix on AWS (via Terraform)

Frontend: Next.js with TypeScript

Don’t worry if you haven’t worked with these technologies before - we value strong fundamentals and curiosity over specific tools.

If you’ve worked with languages like JavaScript, Python, Java, Go, or Rust , you’ll be able to pick things up quickly.
